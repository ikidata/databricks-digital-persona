How It Works

This app analyzes your Databricks system tables (system.access.audit, system.query.history, system.compute.clusters, system.lakeflow.jobs, system.access.table_lineage, and more) to build a comprehensive profile of who you are on the platform. It classifies you into one of 6 persona archetypes, captures your work patterns, SQL expertise, data domains, collaboration network, and more. The analysis only reads your own data — filtered by your user email.
